Pereira Aragüete Lawyers

Pereira Aragüete Lawyers was born in 1990 and immediately stands out winning the rights of health workers in Extremadura who are currently struggling for the recognition of their profession. Alfredo Pereira is associated with many other professionals in the sector, but always with his signature as a reference, from there, the trajectory has been unstoppable and grows exponentially every year. Nowadays a great team of specialists in the great majority of the sectors of law, a personalized service to the client and a list of experts in which it supports its causes and its own specialized investigators in all sectors, are currently consolidating their work; giving service throughout the Spanish territory and throughout Europe. Alfredo Pereira Aragüete has an important influence in the field of teaching since he assists the future lawyers in the UEX law master's degree To his credit Alfredo Pereira Aragüete and his firm Pereira Aragüete Abogados collaborates with the FFCC of Security in the training and recycling of its components in terms of legislation as well as innumerable technical presentations in different entities, estates and universities throughout Spain.
